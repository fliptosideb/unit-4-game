The D.E.N.N.I.S. System

How you play:

The waitress can be negged as certain amount ("Amount to neg").

Charlie has four different approaches to negging.

By clicking on a Charlie below, you will add a specific amount of negs to your neg total ("Amount negged").

Charlie gets a date with the waitress by negging her the exact amount.

Charlie gets rejected by the waitress if he over-negs.

The amount Charlie negs is hidden from you until you click on a Charlie.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the amount each Charlie negs.
